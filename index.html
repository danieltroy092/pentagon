The process

Free Consultation

At our initial visit / call, we will introduce you to our company and network.
We always work closely with our network and governing body to ensure a smooth and worry free experience for all of our clients.
We'll chat about your current lifestyle and financial priorities, working with you to provide the most suitable and cost effective protection.

Recommendation

The information that you provide, will enable us to make a full recommendation to meet your demands and needs.
This will give you enough information to for you to make an informed decision of what insurance you may need to protect your future.
Our recommendation will be easy to understand and with one main goal; To keep you and your family fully protected 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Aftercare

We all make the mistake of not reviewing our insurances on a regular basis; At Pentagon we do this for you to ensure that you're always getting great value for money.
If a product is launched that we feel will better fit your needs, we will definitely be in touch. We are with our clients for the long term. Insurance changes with your situation, so we urge you to let us know should any circumstances change.
